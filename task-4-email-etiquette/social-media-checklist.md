# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## ✅ Do’s

* Maintain a respectful and positive tone in your posts
* Protect your privacy by limiting personal information
* Share useful and meaningful content
* Use strong passwords and enable security features
* Think before posting and consider long-term impact

## ❌ Don’ts

* Do not share sensitive personal information publicly
* Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
* Do not engage in online arguments or negativity
* Avoid clicking on suspicious links
* Do not overshare details about your personal life
