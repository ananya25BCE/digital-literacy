# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## 🔐 General Safety Tips

* Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
* Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments
* Avoid sharing personal information on public platforms
* Always verify the source before trusting calls or messages
* Keep your apps and devices updated regularly

## 💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips

* Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
* Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on payment apps
* Always double-check the receiver’s name before sending money
* Avoid scanning random QR codes from unknown sources

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime

* Report immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

Act quickly to increase the chances of stopping or recovering fraud.
